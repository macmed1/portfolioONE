Healthy lifestyle App

One of the main feature in the analysis this app provides is that unlike other step-tracking devices, it uses data from your IPhone built-in accelerometer (data based on the speed and movement of your phone)to deliver a in depth description of your daily progress and gives you suggestions on how to proceed.

Since this app is web based it requires to sync data from your I phone app to your computer so that it can deliver its analysis.

The main objective is to make the data more user friendly , in addition to a more precise and tailored health tips and suggestions. A better understanding of your progress on a daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly base reinforces more performing healthier lifestyles habits.
